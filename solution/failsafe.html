Solution: Failsafe
Answer: SCHNAPPS

Written by Todd Becker

This puzzle is about spinning safe dials with letters on them, according to given combinations. When we match a dial with a combination, we get a list of ingredients (with an extra letter) for a well-known cocktail. For confirmation, the cocktails are in alphabetical order by the given turn sequences.
