{% translate "Solving" %}

  • {% blocktranslate %}Each answer is a string of letters A–Z; or digits 0-9. Lowercase letters will be changed into uppercase, and any other characters will be stripped before checking for correctness.{% endblocktranslate %}
  • {% blocktranslate with max_guesses=max_guesses_per_puzzle %}You have {{ max_guesses }} total guesses for each puzzle. If you legitimately run out of guesses and need more, feel free to contact us, though we won’t grant your request if we see that you were, for example, guessing random thematic answers, or brute-forcing all 10-letter words.{% endblocktranslate %}
  • {% blocktranslate %}Some puzzles have intermediate answer checking. This means that you may get confirmation for subpuzzle answers or cluephrases. Not every step will necessarily be checkable. Puzzles with intermediate checking will be noted accordingly. Correct intermediate answer submissions will not be counted towards the guess limit.{% endblocktranslate %}
