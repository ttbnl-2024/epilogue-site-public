We find the original Burma-Shave slogan, and as instructed by the last limerick, note the unique word common to both the limerick and the slogan.

This is a cryptic clue, "Change up soap and so on without a sipper (4 5)". The wordplay is "change" indicating an anagrind of "up soap, so on", and "without a" to subtract A. The definition is "sipper". This resolves to the final answer SOUP SPOON. {% endblock %}
